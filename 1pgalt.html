About AandA Fragrances

AandA Fragrances was founded by Adam Meherali and Adam Onufer, two creators dedicated to crafting bold, dark, and expressive 3ml cologne decants. Built on passion, precision, and late-night experimentation, AandA delivers luxury vibes without luxury prices.

All pickups are done in person — we do not ship. No custom blends, but you can request specific fragrances for an upcharge.

Core Collection

JPG Le Beau Le Parfum

Le Beau Le Parfum by Jean Paul Gaultier is an Oriental Woody fragrance for men. Launched in 2022. Top notes: Pineapple, Iris, Ginger, Cypress. Middle: Coconut, Woodsy Notes. Base: Tonka Bean, Sandalwood, Amber, Ambergris.

JPG Le Male Elixir

Le Male Elixir by Jean Paul Gaultier is an Oriental Fougere fragrance for men. Launched in 2023. Top: Lavender, Mint. Middle: Vanilla, Benzoin. Base: Honey, Tonka Bean, Tobacco.

Azzaro The Most Wanted Parfum

A warm, spicy, sweet, amber fragrance with woody undertones. Opens with cardamom, moves through toffee, settles into amberwood. Strong projection and 6+ hour performance.

Maison Margiela Replica By The Fireplace

A warm & spicy unisex fragrance that evokes the cozy feeling of a winter fireplace. Launched in 2015.

Nautica Voyage

Fresh, clean, oceanic fragrance with green apple, lotus, cedar, musk, and amber. Great for warm weather, gym, and casual wear.

Emporio Armani Stronger With You Intensely

Sweet, vanilla, amber, cinnamon fragrance. Cozy winter scent with strong projection and 6+ hour performance.

Valentino Born in Roma Intense

Warm, sweet lavender with ginger sparks and a deep amber-tonka-musk drydown. Rich, sweet, and slightly powdery.
