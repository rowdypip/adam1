JPG Le Beau Le Parfum

Le Beau Le Parfum by Jean Paul Gaultier is a Oriental Woody fragrance for men. Le Beau Le Parfum was launched in 2022. The nose behind this fragrance is Quentin Bisch. Top notes are Pineapple, Iris, Ginger and Cypress; middle notes are Coconut and Woodsy Notes; base notes are Tonka Bean, Sandalwood, Amber and Ambergris.

JPG Le Male Elixir

Le Male Elixir by Jean Paul Gaultier is a Oriental Fougere fragrance for men. Le Male Elixir was launched in 2023. The nose behind this fragrance is Quentin Bisch. Top notes are Lavender and Mint; middle notes are Vanilla and Benzoin; base notes are Honey, Tonka Bean and Tobacco.

Azarro The Most Wanted Parfum

Azzaro The Most Wanted is a warm, spicy, sweet, and amber perfume with woody undertones. It opens with cardamom, transitions through toffee, and settles into a warm base of amberwood. The fragrance is known for its good projection and performance, lasting over 6 hours. It is designed for confident men who want to make a bold statement with their fragrance choice.

Maison Margiela Replica By The Fireplace

REPLICA By The Fireplace is a warm & spicy unisex fragrance by Maison Margiela that launched in 2015. The scent evokes the enveloping sensation of a cozy fireplace in the midst of winter.

Nautica Voyage

Nautica Voyage is a men's fragrance that captures the essence of the ocean with its fresh and invigorating profile. The top notes of green apple and leaf create a crisp and clean introduction, while the heart features mimosa and lotus, adding a soft and lightly floral touch. The base notes of cedar, musk, moss, and amber provide a warm and masculine finish, enhancing the overall composition. This fragrance is particularly popular during the warmer months for its refreshing and clean scent, making it suitable for casual wear, gym sessions, and even dates

Emporio Armani Stronger With You Intensely

Emporio Armani Stronger With You Intensely by Giorgio Armani is vanilla, sweet, amber perfume with cinnamon undertones. Perfect winter fragrance with a cozy feeling, darker and more intense with a sweet, chocolatey, caramely scent, great new scent for gourmand lovers. It opens with juniper, pink pepper and violet, transitions through heart notes of cinnamon, lavender, sage and toffee, and settles into a warm base of amber, suede, tonka bean and vanilla. Good Projection and performance is above average 6+ hours.

valentino born in roma intense

The Valentino Born In Roma Intense cologne features a rich and complex scent profile that evolves from a sweet and floral coalition to a deep, sweet aura with woody and powdery traces. The fragrance opens with vanilla and lavender, providing a sweet and floral coalition with aromatic touches. As it develops, ginger adds an energetic kick, and the scent settles into a deep, sweet aura with amber, tonka, and musk. The drydown reveals a comforting musk with intermittent sweet whiffs of amber and a faint tonka-like woody sweetness. Overall, it is described as warm, sweet lavender with occasional zingy sparks, transitioning into a deep, sweet aura with woody and powdery traces.
